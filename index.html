Who We Are

A home-grown company, building for how Bruneians actually live.

ByteWave Digital Enterprise designs mobile platforms made for Brunei first. We're starting with two products — gig work and tourism — because they sit at the center of two things Bruneians care about every day: earning well, and enjoying home.

We're still early. Both products are in active development. But the direction is clear: build the digital infrastructure Brunei needs, get it right at home, then take it further across the region.
